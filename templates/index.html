Change for Change

"One penny may seem to you a very insignificant thing, but it is the small
seed from which fortunes spring" - Orison Swett Marden

What Are We?

Change for Change is a revolutionary way of supporting your favorite charity.
We are an application that rounds up your daily expenses, collects the difference, and
then stores it away for future donations. Our automated system ensures continuous
donations so you no longer have to worry about not donating enough.

How to Get Started?

  • 1. Create an account
  • 2. Connect your bank account to your Change for Change account
  • 3. Browse through our carefully curated list of charities and select the ones you'd like to support
  • 4. That's it! Sit back and relax while our automated donation system takes care of the rest
